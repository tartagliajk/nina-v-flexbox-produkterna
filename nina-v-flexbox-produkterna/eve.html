    Eve (also stylized as E ve, born May 23, 1995) is a singer-songwriter and Vocaloid producer. He got his start doing covers on Nico Nico Douga. He signed to Toy's Factory in 2019. He was also a guest in "School of Lock!" by Tokyo FM. His music has been featured in the anime Dororo, Jujutsu Kaisen and Josee, the Tiger and the Fish. He started his career on Japanese video-sharing service Niconico in October 2009. He has a manga, Kara no Kioku, illustrated by Newo.
