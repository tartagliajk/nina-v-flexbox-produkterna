Rkive Studios as a concept was founded in 2013 in a small city in Sweden and has in a short time grown to today be one of World's leading retailers in Asian music. The concept of success has always been to constantly expand and think big, while at the same time keeping a firm grip on our roots as music. As a customer with us, you can always feel confident that we have a passion for music that allows us to help you make the right decision. To date, Rkive has 13 physical stores spread around the globe, sales via mail order and online store and approx. 1000 employees within the company.
